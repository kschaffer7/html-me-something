Kirsti & Cars!

I grew up in a family that absolutley loves cars.
Just like Marisa Tomei's character in the movie "My Cousin Vinny", my father was a mechanic, his father was a mechanic, my brother is a mechanic, all my friends are mechanics...
So, naturally I enjoy cars and making mechanical and subtle appearance changes to them.


My First Car - 1993 Honda Civic ® Hatchback

My first car was the 1993 Honda Civic ® Hatchback.
These cars became extremely popular throughout the 2000's thanks to the movie "The Fast and The Furious".
These cars were very cheap to own and maintain, but also very cheap to modify.

My Second Car - 2006 Subaru Impreza WRX ®

My second car was the 2006 Subaru Impreza WRX ®.
These cars were and still are popular amoung car enthusiasts due to their heritage in rally racing.
The WRX is extremely fast and agile on any surface due to its all wheel drive and turbo boxer engine.


My Third Car - 2012 Ford Mustang GT ®

My third and current car is a 2012 Mustang GT ®.
The Mustang has been a wildly popular performance car in America for many years.

Cars are more than just a machine...

Cars are a big part of what keeps my friends and family engaged with each other.
We go to the racetrack together, car shows, and work together to keep each others cars at their best.
